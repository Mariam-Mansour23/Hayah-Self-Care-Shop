about us

Our story

Hayah started as a little idea in front of a mirror — one breakout, a mismatched product, and a “why is skincare so complicated?” moment. We wanted more than just pretty packaging and impossible promises. We wanted real results, real ingredients, and a real glow. So we created Hayah — a brand built on the belief that skincare should feel like self-love, not a science project. What began as a passion for clean, feel-good beauty quickly turned into a mission to help others fall in love with their skin, just as it is — no filters needed.

Our mission

Our mission is simple: to help you glow with confidence.We are here to make skincare fun, easy, and full of intention. We believe beauty should celebrate individuality, and your skin deserves products that are kind, nourishing, and made with love. Whether you are getting ready for a night out or winding down with a candle-lit mask, Hayah is your everyday companion for soft, radiant, happy skin.
